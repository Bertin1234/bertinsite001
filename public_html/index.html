What is Blys

To be truthful Blys is many things. Blys is how I think, Blys is also a project that I have created to correctly represent myself to basically anyone. Blys is not only set in stone nor is it only word of mouth. To call Blys a company would cheapen Blys but, if you can only understand it as a company, I would not hate you for thinking that. Blys is about bringing myself to your level of thinking in an artistic way. Weather that level of thinking is branding, website design, or just a plain old illustration.

My style and what it is

My skills range from a variety of programs, but I am very comfortable with every program in the Adobe Creative Suite. Also I am trying to verge the gap between programming and design by teaching myself such languages like Java script, and HTML. I also have traditional art skills, drawing and pen and ink illustrations are what I am most comfortable with.

Experience with the field.

At the time I am a freelance graphic designer mostly specializing in logo designs, web-page layouts and stationary. Being a freelance graphic designer infused me with a lot of experience about the industry.
