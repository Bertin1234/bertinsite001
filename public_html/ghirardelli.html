Description

The Ghirardelli Product Label project was a school assignment piece that was created over five years ago.The guidelines of the project was that the style of the product had to go with the era of time the product existed. Since the product existed in the early 20s I chose the 1920s as the time and era and the style I chose was art deco. All in all my professor and the students loved the piece.
